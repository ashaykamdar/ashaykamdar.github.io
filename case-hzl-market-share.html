Regaining Market Share at Hindustan Zinc (HZL)

Problem

  • Over-reliance on direct OEM sales
  • Limited channel reach
  • No structured channel partner ecosystem

Objective

Increase market share by 5% within 6 months.

Role

Business Analyst – CMO’s Office

Solution & Strategy

  • Launched Pan-India Channel Partner Program
  • Shifted to hybrid OEM + CP sales model
  • Built a weighted CP selection framework

Post-Selection Analytics

  • Region-wise sales analysis
  • B2B vs B2C split
  • New vs repeat customer tracking
  • Leakage & concentration risk analysis

Impact

  • +5% market share in 6 months
  • ~₹1,000 Cr incremental business
  • Scalable & governed channel ecosystem
