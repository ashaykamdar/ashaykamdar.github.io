Improving CSAT via Digital Sales Platform at HZL

Problem

Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) had fallen below 60% due to fragmented customer journeys across sales, logistics, and finance. Customers faced delayed invoices, no vehicle visibility, manual ledger reconciliation, and no real-time pricing or payment clarity.

Objective

Fix the end-to-end customer experience and rebuild trust without increasing operational overhead.

Role

Business Analyst – CMO’s Office

Solution

I helped launch India’s first mobile + web digital sales platform for non-ferrous metals, unifying sales, logistics, and finance into a single experience.

  • Digital ledger and invoices in a few clicks
  • IoT-enabled live GPS vehicle tracking
  • JP Morgan API for live LME prices and instant booking
  • Real-time payment status visibility
  • Centralized, searchable MoM tracking

Impact

  • CSAT improved by 37% within 6 months
  • Significant reduction in complaints and follow-ups
  • Created a scalable digital sales foundation
