Improving CSAT via Digital Sales Platform at HZL

Problem

  • Delayed invoices & documents
  • No live vehicle tracking
  • Manual ledger reconciliation
  • No target-price booking
  • No real-time payment visibility
  • Fragmented MoM communication

Objective

Fix end-to-end customer experience across sales, logistics, and finance.

Role

Business Analyst – CMO’s Office

Solution

  • Launched India’s first mobile + web digital sales platform for non-ferrous metals
  • Integrated digital ledger & invoices
  • IoT-enabled live GPS vehicle tracking
  • JP Morgan API for live LME pricing & instant booking
  • Real-time payment status visibility
  • Centralized customer-wise MoM tracking

Impact

  • CSAT increased by 37% within 6 months
  • Significant reduction in customer complaints
  • Established scalable digital sales foundation
