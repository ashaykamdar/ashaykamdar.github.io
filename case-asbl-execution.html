Improving Execution Visibility on a B2B SaaS Platform at ASBL

Context

ASBL used a B2B SaaS platform to track on-ground construction execution. However, PMO teams lacked trust in the data due to poor activity logging by site engineers.

Problem

Data analysis showed that ~70% of activity logs were under 2 minutes, which was operationally impossible. The root causes were systemic:

  • Low network connectivity on sites
  • Too many clicks to start and stop logging
  • No linkage between logging accuracy and KPIs

Objective

Make the platform a single source of truth for execution tracking and decision-making.

My Role

Associate Product Manager

Root Cause Analysis

I downloaded and analyzed activity dumps across projects, engineers, and activity types. The analysis revealed that the issue was systemic, not individual behavior.

Solution

Based on insights, I drove multiple interventions:

  • Launched a lite app version for low-bandwidth environments
  • Reduced steps required to start and stop logging
  • Made accurate logging a formal KPI

Impact

  • ~30% reduction in task completion time
  • Improved data accuracy and adoption
  • Enhanced PMO visibility and execution control
